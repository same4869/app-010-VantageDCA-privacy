VantageDCA Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 13, 2026

Language: English 简体中文 繁體中文

This policy applies to the VantageDCA iOS app. We follow a data-minimization principle and process data only as needed to provide product functionality.

1. Scope

This policy covers your use of DCA plans, tasks, reminders, backtests, and knowledge content inside VantageDCA.

We do not provide custodial trading services and do not place trades on your behalf. All investment decisions and risks remain your responsibility.

2. Local Data Storage

The app stores necessary data on your device, including plans, tasks, strategy parameters, language/theme preferences, and reminder settings.

This data is used for continuity and feature availability, and is not uploaded to our own backend by default.

3. iCloud Sync (Optional)

When iCloud sync is enabled, plan and task data is written to your iCloud/CloudKit container for cross-device sync and restore.

iCloud data is governed by your Apple account and Apple privacy policies. If you disable this feature, iCloud sync stops.

4. Third-Party Services and Permissions

To provide market and temperature data, the app requests third-party data APIs. Those providers may receive standard connection metadata (such as IP) from network requests.

Notifications, calendar sync, and email reminders are optional features and require your explicit permission or enablement. You can revoke permissions in iOS Settings at any time.

5. Data Security

We rely on iOS sandboxing and system protections to secure local data and reduce exposure.

We recommend enabling a device passcode, Apple ID two-factor authentication, and protecting accounts that can access your device or iCloud.

6. Your Controls

You can edit or delete plans and tasks, disable notifications/calendar/iCloud, and remove local data by uninstalling the app.

If iCloud is enabled, deletion and restoration behavior also follows iCloud sync rules.

7. Policy Updates

We may update this policy when product capabilities or compliance requirements change, and publish the latest version in the app.

Please refer to the version indicated by the “Last updated” date.

8. Contact

If you have questions about this policy, please contact us via the project repository issue tracker or product feedback channels.
